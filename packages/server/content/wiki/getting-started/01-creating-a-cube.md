To build a cube you'll first need a Cube Cobra account. If you don't have one yet, use the
**Register** link in the top navigation to sign up for a free account.

## Make a new cube

Creating a cube takes one click: open the **Your Cubes** menu in the top navigation and choose
**Create A New Cube**. (If you don't have any cubes yet, your dashboard offers the same thing.)

The cube is created immediately, named "*your username*'s New Cube", and you land on its list page
with the editing panel already open, ready for cards. A couple of limits keep things tidy: you can
only have two empty cubes at a time, and up to 256 cubes in total.

## Make it yours

A new cube starts public, with a randomly chosen cover image and a placeholder description. Click
the pencil icon next to the cube's name to set your own name, a custom short URL, a cover image,
and a category. If you'd rather build in private before showing the world, the visibility setting
is under **Settings → Options**. Every setting is covered in [Settings](/wiki/cube-page/settings).

When you're ready to tell drafters what the cube is about, write a primer on the **About** tab. It
supports full Cube Cobra Markdown, card links and images included; see
[About: Primer, Blog, and Changelog](/wiki/cube-page/about).
