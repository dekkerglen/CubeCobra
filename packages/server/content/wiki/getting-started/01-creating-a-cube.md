To build a cube you'll first need a Cube Cobra account. If you don't have one yet, use the
**Register** link in the top navigation to sign up — it's free.

## Make a new cube

1. Log in and open **Your Cubes** from the navigation menu.
2. Click **Create a New Cube**.
3. Give your cube a name. You can change this later at any time.

Your new cube starts empty and is ready for cards.

## Cube settings

Open your cube and go to the **Settings** tab to configure it:

- **Visibility** — whether the cube is public (listed and searchable) or unlisted.
- **Category and tags** — help others find your cube and describe what it is (e.g. _Legacy_,
  _Peasant_, _Powered_).
- **Default draft format** — the format used when someone starts a draft without picking one.
- **Price visibility** — whether estimated card prices are shown.

## Write a description

The **Overview** page supports full Markdown, including card links and images. For example, typing
`[[Sol Ring]]` links the card with a hover preview, and `[[!Sol Ring]]` embeds its image. See the
[Markdown Guide](/help/markdown) for the complete syntax.
