Welcome to Cube Cobra! This section walks you through the basics of building and managing a cube.

A **cube** is a personalized, curated collection of Magic: The Gathering cards that you and your
friends draft from. Cube Cobra gives you the tools to build your list, keep it organized, and run
drafts and playtests online.

If you're brand new, work through these pages in order:

1. [Creating a Cube](/wiki/getting-started/creating-a-cube)
2. [Adding and Editing Cards](/wiki/getting-started/adding-and-editing-cards)

Once your list is together, head over to [Drafting](/wiki/drafting) to play with it.
