title: Running a Draft

---

Open your cube and go to the **Playtest** tab. From here you can start a draft or a sealed pool.

## Solo draft (against bots)

A solo draft is the fastest way to test your cube. Cube Cobra's bots fill the other seats and make
picks based on card ratings, so you can draft a full deck by yourself in a few minutes.

1. On the **Playtest** tab, choose a draft format.
2. Click **Start Draft**.
3. Make your picks. When the draft finishes, you're taken to the deckbuilder.

## Drafting with friends

To draft with a group, start a draft and share the lobby link. Other players join the same lobby,
and the draft begins once everyone is seated. Empty seats can be filled by bots.

## Draft formats

A cube can define multiple **custom draft formats** that control pack size, number of packs, and how
packs are built (for example, guaranteeing a certain number of lands or cards of each color). Manage
these in your cube's format settings. If no custom format is selected, the cube's default format is
used.

## After the draft: the deckbuilder

When drafting ends you land in the **deckbuilder**, where you split your pool into a maindeck and
sideboard. Cube Cobra suggests a curve and automatically adds basic lands. From here you can:

- Save the deck to share it or come back later.
- Playtest the deck in the **sample hand** and goldfish tools.
- Export the deck to other formats.

## Sealed

Prefer sealed? The **Playtest** tab also offers a sealed pool — Cube Cobra generates a set of packs,
opens them all at once, and drops you straight into the deckbuilder with the full pool.
