Cube Cobra lets you draft your cube online — solo against bots to test your list, or with a group of
friends. This section covers how to get a draft going.

- [Running a Draft](/wiki/drafting/running-a-draft)

Drafting is the best way to pressure-test a cube: it surfaces archetypes that don't have enough
support, colors that are over- or under-drafted, and cards that never make anyone's deck.
